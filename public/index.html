About Us

Tracker is a nutritional tracking app where you can choose your nutrition plan and track your daily food consumption. You don’t need an expensive gym membership to stay fit. The app can help you choose your goals and track your daily intake. Filters and search options help you narrow down your choices so that you can book the workout that’s just right for you. At Tracker, we focus on BMI tracking, Macros, total weekly calories count, suggested meal plans and much more. Because the easier we make it for you, the more likely you are to stay on track. .
